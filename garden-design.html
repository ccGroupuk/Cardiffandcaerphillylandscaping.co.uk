Our Design Process

A structured approach to creating your perfect garden.

1. Consultation

We meet on-site to discuss your needs, budget, and vision. We assess the space, soil conditions, and light levels to understand the potential of your garden.

Book Visit

2. Concept Design

Our designers create initial sketches and mood boards. We present different layout options and material palettes to help you visualize the transformation.

Learn More

3. Detailed Plans

Once a concept is approved, we produce detailed planting plans, construction drawings, and lighting layouts ready for the build team.

View Examples

4. The Build

Our experienced landscapers bring the plan to life. We handle all aspects of construction, from groundworks and paving to fencing and planting.
