A Homeowner’s Guide to Hiring a Landscaper

Essential advice on costs, vetting, and your rights. We believe in transparency and honest service.

Understanding Costs

£40 - £60 Standard Hourly Rate
  • Day rates typically around £350.
  • Expect premium fees for Emergency Call-Outs (£100-£120).

Vetting Checklist

  • Marshalls Register: Mandatory for any gas appliance work.
  • Public Liability Insurance: Protects your property from damage.
  • Written Guarantee: Clarifies warranty on parts and labour.

Your Legal Rights

  • Consumer Rights Act 2015: Protection for up to 6 years.
  • Right to Redress: Legally request a re-do or discount if work isn't up to standard.

Regional Averages

Typical hourly rates by region:

£40-90 Wales & SW
£60-90 London & SE

Check Reviews

Check Google, Trustpilot, and social feeds for consistent feedback.

  • Look for recent, genuine reviews.

The Person Check

  • Appearance: Is the Landscaper dressed professionally?
  • Vehicle: Do they have a sign-written van?
  • Tools: Do they have the right tools for the job?
